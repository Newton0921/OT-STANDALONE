# Lab Briefing: Blue Participant

**Target Sector:** Energy / Electric Sector
**Objective:** Detect and Analyze Process Parameter Modification

**Scenario:**
You are an OT SOC analyst responding to a critical `DeviationAlarm` generated by a local generator's voltage-control system. The physical process was halted after a reactive-power parameter unexpectedly spiked, breaching safe operational bands.

You have been provided with raw OPC UA application logs, including session tracking, write events, value changes, and process alarms. Your objective is to forensically reconstruct the incident: identify the unauthorized session, determine the precise node modification, establish how the bypass occurred, and document the resulting alarm timestamp.

*Context: MITRE ATT&CK for ICS T0836 (Modify Parameter) & TA0106 (Impair Process Control).*

# Scenario: Ghost in the Logic

You are an OT Security Analyst. Operators at the distribution center have reported an anomaly: Feeder 3 is intermittently shedding its load, disrupting non-critical downstream processes. Mechanical inspections show no physical faults.

**Your Objective:**
Investigate the primary PLC (OpenPLC Runtime). Suspecting a persistence mechanism and unauthorized program modification (T0889), you must obtain the active project logic, compare it against your known-good engineering baseline, pinpoint the exact structural changes, and trace the origin of the modification through system logs. Document all technical artifacts thoroughly.
